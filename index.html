I have been a writer with universities, nonprofits, and newspapers for a greater part of my career. My work has imbued me with a strong sense of commitment to the written word.

I have a propensity to work in culturally diverse, intellectually curious, and creative environments. Over the years, my published stories have drawn significant coverage while reflecting a sense of purpose and a distinct essence of the organizational voice.

I live in the San Francisco Bay Area while I work at UC Davis.
