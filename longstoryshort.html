Long Story Short

Living Without Libraries

Living Without Libraries is a short documentary film that showcases the relationship between the public library and its community during the COVID-19 shutdown. Using in-person and virtual interviews, the film tries to capture the sentiments and values around public libraries, as well as offers a glimpse into the stories of some of the community members coping with the crisis.

The people interviewed for the film reflect on the good times when a visit to the library was a joyous activity, and how its closure has now affected them.

A short documentary production by: Neelanjana Gautam

Music: Bensound

Archival footage: Sunnyvale Public Library, King Library
